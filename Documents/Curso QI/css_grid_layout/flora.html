Acid Mushroom
Acid Mushrooms are a hazardous species of flora. They are used as a raw material to manufacture Batteries.
Bloodroot
Bloodroots are a flora species that dominate the Blood Kelp Caves. They frequently feature Blood Oil pustules, which are a harvestable raw material that is essential for use in Benzene.
Bloodvine
Bloodvines are a flora species that dominate the Blood Kelp Zone. An oily substance called Blood Oil can be harvested from their stems. Bloodroot is most likely the roots of Bloodvine.
Blue Palm
Blue Palms are a purely decorative flora species found mostly in shallower biomes. Using a knife, the player can harvest Blue Palm Seeds from it, which can then be planted.
Bulb Bush
Bulb Bushes are a fast-spreading flora species found exclusively in the Bulb Zone. Four different sizes can be found: Pygmy, Common, Large, and Giant. While the Common, Large, and Giant Bulb Bushes are only found in the Bulb Zone, Pygmy Bulb Bushes can be found both in the Bulb Zone and by planting a Bulb Bush in an Exterior Growbed or Alien Containment. Bulb Bushes can be harvested for their edible samples by using the knife. It also can be used in other various crafting recipes.
Bulbo Tree
Bulbo Trees are a terrestrial flora species found on the Floating Island and Mountain Island. The player can harvest Bulbo Tree Samples from them using a Survival Knife. Bulbo Tree Samples are edible, and a viable source of both Food & Water. Each individual slash with the Survival Knife yields one sample, until the 9th slash which instead yields two samples and destroys the tree.
Cave Bush
Cave Bushes are a flora species found populating the Jellyshroom Caves, Mountains Caves, and the Sea Treader's Path. It is purely decorative. By using a knife, the player can harvest Cave Bush Seeds.
Chinese Potato Plant
Chinese Potato Plants are a non-native, terrestrial flora species found only in Exterior Growbeds and Plant Pots in and around the Floating Island Degasi Seabase. It is both plantable and scannable, and the source of the Chinese Potato.
Creepvine
Creepvine is a flora species which grows exclusively within Kelp Forests. By cutting a Creepvine with a Knife, the player can acquire Creepvine Samples. Repeated knife strikes will eventually destroy the plant completely. The player can also collect Creepvine Seed Clusters from Creepvines that have them growing.
Deep Shroom
The Deep Shroom is a hazardous flora species found in abundance in the Blood Kelp Zones and their caves, in small clusters, and in the Lost River and the Sea Treader's Tunnel Caves. It is a relative of the Acid Mushroom, and is used as a raw material in the manufacturing of Hydrochloric Acid.
Eye Stalk
The Eye Stalk is a Flora species found scattered throughout a few of the deeper biomes, as well as the Kelp Forest Caves. It is mostly decoration for the game, being used in only one crafting recipe. It can be harvested for Eye Stalk Seeds by using the knife.
Fern Palm
The Fern Palm is a likely bio-engineered, terrestrial flora species. It can be harvested for Fern Palm Seeds with a knife, but is purely decorative.
Furled Papyrus
The Furled Papyrus is a flora species mainly found in shallower biomes.
Gabe's Feather
The Gabe's Feather is a flora species found mainly in the Blood Kelp Zone. The Gabe's Feather Seed can be obtained by using a knife.
Gel Sack
The Gel Sack is an edible flora species that can also be used to craft Aerogel.
Ghost Weed
The Ghost Weed is a flora species found in deep biomes. The ghost weed is most commonly found in the Lost River but can be found in Blood Kelp Zone and Bulb Zone too. It can be harvested for Ghost Weed Seeds with a knife.
Grub Basket
The Grub Basket is a terrestrial flora species only found on the Floating Island. It is only cosmetic and has no other use, except to be used as fuel in the Bioreactor. Using a knife, the player can harvest Grub Basket Seeds from Grub Baskets.
Jaffa Cup
The Jaffa Cup is a terrestrial flora species found on the Floating Island.
Jellyshroom
The Jellyshroom is a giant, highly luminescent flora species found only in the Jellyshroom Cave. Some Jellyshrooms are home to the Crabsnakes. Crabsnakes live inside the hollow trunks, and will spring out to ambush the player.
Lantern Tree
The Lantern Tree is a rare, terrestrial flora species found in a small section towards the back of the Floating Island, near a Degasi seabase as well as a single growbed inside one of the abandoned observatories.
Marblemelon Plant
The Marblemelon Plant is a terrestrial life form belonging to the flora category. By using a Survival Knife, the player can harvest Marblemelon Seeds from Marblemelons. Marblemelons can be eaten for water and nutrition, and is an effective food source when farmed. Once planted from seed, the Marblemelon will take 800s (13m:20s) to mature.
Membrain Tree
The Membrain Tree is difficult to categorize, as it is composed of fauna and coral species living in symbiosis, though the PDA places it in the flora category. It is one of the dominant plants in the Grand Reef, along with Anchor Pods. It is mostly found attached on rock ledges in the biome. The Membrain Tree also grows along the Sea Treader's Path.
Ming Plant
The Ming Plant is a flora species found exclusively on the Floating Island. By using the knife to slice it, the player can harvest Ming Plant Seeds from it.
Pink Cap
The Pink Cap is a terrestrial flora species found in abundance on the Floating Island, and the Mountain Island.
Purple Pinecone
The Purple Pinecone is a very rare flora species that acts as a harvesting node. It can be harvested for salt deposits without the use of any tools. Two can always be located southeast from Wreck 9 in the Northwestern Mushroom Forest. Purple Pinecones are unable to be scanned, and therefore have no databank entry.
Pygmy Fan
The Pygmy Fan is a small, relatively rare flora species mostly found scarcely populating the Mushroom Forest and Bulb Zone in small clusters.
Redwort
Redwort is a flora species found in numerous shallow biomes. By cutting it with the knife, the player can obtain Redwort Seeds.
Regress Shell
The Regress Shell is a flora species that is generally found in deeper biomes. It is purely decorative.
Rouge Cradle
The Rouge Cradle is a large flora species. It is purely decorative and practical only as a dim light source around Seabases.
Sea Crown
The Sea Crown is one of the rarest types of harvestable flora in Subnautica, with only a small number in existence. Overall, it is the third rarest type of flora in Subnautica, behind the Giant Cove Tree and Fern Palm. It is found exclusively in the Bulb Zone, the Dunes Sinkhole, the entrance to the Jellyshroom caves, in the Sea emperor leviathan's aquarium, and certain Grassy Plateaus Caves. One of these plants can be found in the general vicinity of Lifepod 12.
Speckled Rattler
The Speckled Rattler is a small, terrestrial flora species. It is found in abundance on the Floating Island.
Spiked Horn Grass
Spiked Horn Grass is a mid-sized decorative flora species. By striking it with a knife, Spiked Horn Grass Seeds can be obtained.
Spotted Dockleaf
The Spotted Dockleaf is a flora species found growing abundantly in caves, particularly in those of the Bulb Zone.
Sulfur Plant
The Sulfur Plant is a flora species that lives in a symbiotic relationship with the Crashfish.
Tiger Plant
The Tiger Plant is a hazardous flora species. It is commonly found in rocky areas of the Grassy Plateaus and its caves, while also being present on the backs of adult Reefback Leviathans and among the clusters of Eye Stalks in the Sparse Reef.
Veined Nettle
The Veined Nettle is a Flora species found inhabiting shallower Biomes. Using a Survival Knife, the player can harvest Veined Nettle Seeds from it, which can then be planted. It is commonly found in the Safe Shallows and sometimes in the Grassy Plateaus or Mushroom Forest. The Veined Nettle is mainly for decoration purposes only.
Violet Beau
The Violet Beau is a purely decorative species of flora, found inhabiting a wide range of biomes. By slashing at the plant with the knife, the player can obtain Violet Beau Seeds.
Voxel Shrub
The Voxel Shrub is a terrestrial flora species. By using a knife on it, the player can harvest Voxel Shrub Seeds, which can then be planted.
Writhing Weed
Writhing Weed is a flora species found mainly in shallower biomes.
Anchor Pods
Anchor Pods are a large flora species found exclusively in the Grand Reef and Deep Grand Reef. Anchor Pods serve only as decorations.
Blood Grass
Blood Grass is a flora species found exclusively in the Grassy Plateaus in great abundance, which in turn derives the biome's name from it.
Brine Lily
The Brine Lily is a somewhat rare type of Flora that is only found in a corridor of the Lost River, where they can be found in groups floating on the surface of brine pools. Brine Lilies cannot be harvested.
Crab Claw Kelp
Crab Claw Kelp is a flora species found growing exclusively in Lost River's Bone Fields and Ghost Canyon, protruding upwards out of the brine pools.
Drooping Stingers
Drooping Stingers are a hazardous Flora species which mainly grow in caves, where they can be found attached to the ceiling. They can also be found hanging in the southwest Kelp Forest.
Giant Cove Tree
The Giant Cove Tree is a unique species of Flora. The only specimen known to exist can be found within the Tree Cove of the Lost River. All of the brine around the tree is blue in color, rather than the green brine found elsewhere in the Lost River; whether or not the Giant Cove Tree is the cause for this alteration in color is unknown.
Tree Leech
The Tree Leech is a small species of flora found growing on the side of Tree Mushrooms or on the walls of the Mushroom Forest Caves and the Mountains Caves.
